No-Sleepover Registration for East Coast Rationalist Megameetup 2017

If you want to be guaranteed a spot at the Megameetup (assuming it goes forward) but don’t intend to sleep at the Airbnb location, register here (instead of with the main form) by October 30. You’ll be asked for a $75 authorization (instead of $125), and on October 30 you’ll be charged half as much for the rental as participants who’ll be sleeping over, plus the $25 for other expenses.

Please be aware that after October 30, participants who aren’t sleeping over will be allowed to register for only $25—but only if there’s room for them in the location that’s already been rented. By registering here and now at the higher price, you ensure that there’ll be a space for you.

All remaining questions are optional.

For the next three questions, it’s fine if you don’t want to commit to anything right now; just say you’re not sure and you’ll be asked again in mid-to-late November.

You’ll provide your email address on the next screen.
